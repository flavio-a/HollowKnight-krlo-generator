    Settings

    Grouping factor should be between 0 and 10.
    This option controls grouping of nearby levers (ie. putting them close to each other in the order). A value of 10 (the maximum) means all close by levers are put together (unless some of them are unreachable on the first trip to the area), while a value of 0 disables this feature.
